About the 2017 bmw i3 :

The 2017 BMW i3 gets a new 94 Ah, 33 kWh battery, which extends range to 114 miles of city/highway use, per BMW. That's a 50 percent increase in stored energy, and a similar increase in all-electric driving range without use of the optional gasoline-powered range extender. Also new for 2017 are a new set of 19-inch wheels, advanced real-time traffic information, a Deka World interior theme, and no-cost DC fast-charging for two years via ChargeNow DC Fast chargers at EVgo stations across the U.S. and it is not available in Egypt . so the price is: 43,395 USD
